Our Policies

Terms and Conditions

Payment is non-refundable apart from if any changes are made at least 7 days before the date of a training event. A refund will not be given if a participant does not take part in the archery session due to non-arrival, late arrival, or being under the influence of alcohol/drugs. Times for archery sessions are fixed, and you will be required to arrive 10 minutes before your allocated start time. If one or more members of the group are late, it may not be possible to incorporate them into the group after instruction has started. No one suspected of being under the influence of alcohol or drugs will be allowed to shoot. Straight to the Point Archery is not liable for any losses or damage to clothing, footwear, or personal effects. It is the responsibility of any participant to inform the instructor if they have any illness, disability, or injury. In the event of a cancellation, Straight to the Point Archery will attempt to contact all attendees provided that we have the correct contact details.

Non-Discrimination Policy

Next Step Archery admits students of any race, gender presentation, sexuality, religion, national origin or ethnic origin to all rights, privileges, programs and activities available to students at the school. We do not discriminate in administration of our educational policies, admission policies and other school administered programs. All students are welcome and valuable members of our community.
